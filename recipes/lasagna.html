Lasagna

Description

Layer cooked lasagna noodles with a meat sauce made from ground beef, crushed tomatoes, and seasonings. Prepare a ricotta mixture with an egg. Assemble the lasagna with alternating layers of noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Bake until bubbly and golden, then let it rest before serving. Enjoy this comforting and flavorful dish!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g/14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (400g/14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance the acidity of tomatoes)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 250g (9 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the noodles and lay them flat on a clean surface or kitchen towel to prevent sticking.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until they become soft and translucent.
    • Add the ground beef and cook until it's browned and cooked through, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, bay leaf, sugar (if using), salt, and black pepper. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally. This allows the flavors to meld together.
  3. Prepare the ricotta mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the egg. Mix until well combined.
  4. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • In a baking dish (approximately 9x13 inches), spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom to prevent sticking.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then add a layer of meat sauce on top.
    • Sprinkle some shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the meat sauce.
    • Repeat the layers: noodles, remaining ricotta mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
    • Finish with a final layer of noodles, meat sauce, and more shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Bake the lasagna:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, tenting it slightly to prevent the cheese from sticking to the foil.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese on top is bubbly and lightly golden.
  6. Let it rest and serve:
    • Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for about 10 minutes before serving. This will make it easier to slice and serve.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.

Enjoy your delicious and hearty Lasagna! Serve it with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
